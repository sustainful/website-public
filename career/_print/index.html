Acquisition Specialist (f/m/d)


Your responsibilities:

  • Acquisition of new customers and software projects in Europe
  • Customer and project pre-selection
  • Presentation of acquired projects and customers to our management team
  • First point of contact for our customers
  • Organisational support of management team
  • Support hiring process
  • Maintenance of our social media channels
  • Bring in creativity, ideas and fun at work

Your qualifications:

  • Business / Sales Education or proven experience in related field
  • Very good communication skills
  • Spanish and English verbal and written fluently, German is a plus
  • Good PowerPoint design and presentation skills

Our preferences:

  • You are determined
  • Having a positive attitude and are open-minded
  • Working autonomously

How does your day look like?

As an Acquisition Specialist, your role is to support our management team with customer and software project acquisitions. Typically, you are making calls, writing emails or social media messages to new potential customers to discuss new opportunities. And you are clarifying project possibilities and requirements. Also you collect and document your success and then you hand off potential new accounts to our management team who attempt to close a deal. Apart you are supporting the management team with organizational tasks. And hopefully you going to join our team activities.
